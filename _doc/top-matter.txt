The following special fields may be used in top-matter in your posts:

image: Path to an image to embedded in open-graph meta-data. Used by
       facebook and others.

attract_image: Image to be displayed as an 'attract' logo on the right,
               of an article title, with rounded corners.

wide_image: Displayed as a 100% wide banner below the image title.
            Also sets `image` for social media.

tags: A space-separated list of tags.

description: A string to be used as the page meta-data description and
             open-graph description.

byline: The byline shown. Defaults to "By <your name>".
